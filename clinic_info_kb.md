1.  **Identify the Core Question:** Carefully read the patient's inquiry to understand the specific information they are seeking.
2.  **Locate the Relevant Information:** Scan this document to find the section that directly addresses the patient's question.
3.  **Formulate the Response:**
    *   **Language Matching:** The response **must** be in the same language as the patient's inquiry. If the question is in Arabic, the answer must be in Arabic. If in English, the answer must be in English.
    *   **Direct and Accurate:** Provide a clear and concise answer based *only* on the information available in this document. Do not add information from external sources or make assumptions.
    *   **Professional Tone:** Maintain a helpful and professional tone at all times.
4.  **Formatting the Answer:**
    *   Start with a direct answer to the question.
    *   If necessary, provide a brief explanation based on the details in this guide.
    *   Do not quote the document directly unless it is a specific term or price. Paraphrase the information into a natural, conversational response.


**Example Question & Answer Format:**


*   **Patient's Question (Arabic):** "كم سعر الكشف عند دكتور ضيدان؟"
*   **Correctly Formatted Answer (Arabic):** "سعر الاستشارة مع دكتور ضيدان هو 50 دينار كويتي، والمراجعة خلال أسبوع تكون مجانية."


*   **Patient's Question (English):** "Do you accept insurance?"
*   **Correctly Formatted Answer (English):** "Yes, we do accept insurance. The consultation fee will be determined by your insurance provider, and your payment will depend on your policy's coverage."


By following these guidelines, we can ensure a high standard of service and clarity in all our patient communications.
