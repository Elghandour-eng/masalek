# descriptions

- `doctors_kb.md`: This tool contains detailed information about the doctors at the Masalek clinic, including their specializations, qualifications, areas of expertise, and working hours. It serves as a comprehensive guide to the medical staff.
- `clinic_info_kb.md`: This document is the official knowledge base for the Masalek clinic's contact center. It is designed to be the single source of truth for all non-medical, operational, and administrative patient inquiries. The information contained herein covers a wide range of topics, including but not limited to: consultation pricing, follow-up appointment policies, insurance and payment procedures, clinic services, appointment scheduling rules, age-specific care, and facility logistics (e.g., pharmacy, lab, radiology). Patient queries should be handled with the utmost care and accuracy, and this guide provides the necessary information to do so. Adhering to these guidelines ensures that patients receive a consistent and high-quality service experience.
- `services_kb.md`: This tool provides detailed information on specialized services, visiting practitioners, and alternative consultation options available at the Masalek clinic. It is a reference tool for the contact center team to accurately answer patient questions on these topics.
